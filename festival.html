Festivals in India

Some of the major festivals celebrated in India are Holi, Diwali, Onam, etc. These festivals may be national or religious.

Diwali

Diwali is the festival of lights, celebrated with lamps, sweets, and fireworks.

Holi

Holi is the festival of colors, marking the arrival of spring and the triumph of good over evil.

Onam

Onam is a harvest festival from Kerala, celebrated with flower carpets and a grand feast.
